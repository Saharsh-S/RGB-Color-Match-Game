HOW TO PLAY

  • Each round shows a Target colour swatch.
  • Drag the R · G · B sliders to recreate it in Your Mix.
  • Hit LOCK IN COLOUR before the timer runs out.
  • Score is based on accuracy — the closer the match, the more points.
  • A speed bonus is added for locking in early.
  • Difficulty increases every round — targets get trickier!
  • Game ends after 10 rounds. Aim for a perfect score!
